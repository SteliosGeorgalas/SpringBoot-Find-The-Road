Welcome!

Our platform has been designed exclusively for organizations that seek complete control of the work that is being performed in their internal processes. Browse our demo to get a sense of its potential.

The basic idea of our platform is to provide in each organization the ability to have universal supervision of all the processes that are implemented within it. For that reason, we have implemented a solution that offers a supervisory way to control the correlations between the various participants, in the organization. Participants in the current version would be employees, employee groups, projects and clients.
